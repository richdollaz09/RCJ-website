From Call to Report

Every Call, Fully Documented

Follow a single call from the moment it comes in — through dispatch, patrol, and the response on scene — all the way to the finished report waiting in your client portal.

01A call comes in
Our dispatch line is answered by a live operator around the clock. Every call is logged with a time, location, and incident type — and automated status texts start going out as it moves.
02Dispatch sends the unit
The nearest available officer is assigned and briefed by radio, with the full call details pushed straight to their patrol vehicle.
03Officers respond on scene
A marked unit arrives, assesses the situation, and takes action — from a welfare check to a full incident response — with GPS confirming arrival.
04Body cameras roll
Every officer wears an Axon body camera, so the response is recorded from arrival to resolution — protecting your property and our accountability.
05Everything gets documented
The officer files a time-stamped report with photos, notes, and the actions taken. No visit goes unrecorded and nothing is left to memory.
06It shows up in your portal
The completed report lands in your client portal, so you can see exactly what happened, when, and how it was handled — any time you want.
