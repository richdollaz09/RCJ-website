About Us

Security Built on Trust, Professionalism and Results

RCJ Protection Agency is a premier private security firm delivering customized protection solutions for individuals, businesses, residential communities, and organizations that demand the highest standards of professionalism. Clients choose RCJ Protection Agency because they want more than a security company—they want a trusted security partner committed to protecting their people, property, and reputation.

As a fully licensed and insured California Private Patrol Operator (PPO), we provide comprehensive security services throughout Southern California, including Los Angeles County, Orange County, Ventura County, the Inland Empire, and surrounding communities. Our Executive Protection services are available nationwide for clients requiring secure travel and specialized protective operations.

Every assignment begins with a clear understanding of our client’s objectives, vulnerabilities, and operational needs. We develop tailored security strategies designed to reduce risk, deter threats, and provide a visible yet professional security presence. Whether protecting a corporate headquarters, luxury residence, private event, or high-profile individual, our focus remains the same: proactive protection, meticulous planning, and exceptional service.

Our officers are carefully selected and professionally trained in defensive tactics, de-escalation, emergency response, threat recognition, and firearms proficiency. They are held to the highest standards of appearance, communication, judgment, and professionalism, ensuring they represent both our company and our clients with distinction.

Everything we do is guided by our five core values
IntegrityProfessionalismCouragePrideLoyalty

These values define our culture, shape every decision we make, and drive our unwavering commitment to excellence.

At RCJ Protection Agency, security is more than standing a post—it is about building confidence, preventing incidents before they occur, and delivering peace of mind through preparation, vigilance, and professionalism. We understand that every client has unique concerns, which is why we provide customized security solutions backed by responsive communication, dependable service, and uncompromising attention to detail.

RCJ Protection Agency is setting the standard for modern private security—where trust is earned, professionalism is expected, and excellence is delivered.
