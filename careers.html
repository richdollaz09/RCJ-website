Why RCJ

Why Work at RCJ Protection Agency

Great security is bottlenecked on great people — and quality people stay where they’re respected.

Modern Equipment

Every officer is issued an Axon body camera, a clean uniform, and access to a well-maintained marked vehicle — the gear is real and it’s yours on day one.

Real Training

New officers complete paid field training before any solo assignment, with ongoing development in de-escalation, reporting, and property-specific protocols.

Consistent Assignments

We structure schedules to keep officers familiar with their properties — you learn the people, routes, and patterns that matter.

Leadership You Can Reach

When something comes up on a shift, there’s a real decision-maker available — not layers of regional managers between you and an answer.

Clear Schedules

We staff to the contracts we have. No hours we can’t deliver, and no last-minute shuffling without warning.

Room to Grow

RCJ has grown every year since 2022 — growth means promotion paths to on-site, supervisor, and dispatch roles.

What We Look For

Who Thrives Here

Private security is honest work that takes real skills. Prior security, law enforcement, or military experience is welcome but not required.

  • Active California BSIS Guard Card (or in process) — required before your first solo shift
  • A clean, felony-free background; we run California DOJ and FBI checks during hiring
  • Valid California driver’s license with a clean record for patrol positions
  • Professional appearance and a de-escalation-first mindset
  • Strong written communication for incident reports and daily activity logs
  • Comfort with body cameras, dispatch systems, and digital reporting
