Company News

Serving Southern California Since 2022

May 2026 · 2 min read

RCJ Protection Agency was founded in 2022 on a straightforward belief: that private security should mean highly trained, professional officers who treat every client’s safety as their own — not clipboard patrols and empty promises.

Built on values

Everything we do is guided by five core values: Integrity, Professionalism, Courage, Pride, and Loyalty. They shape how we hire, how we train, and how we show up on every assignment.

Growing across the region

From executive protection to residential, commercial, patrol, and event security, we have grown to serve communities and businesses across Los Angeles, Orange, Ventura, Riverside, and San Bernardino counties — with executive protection available nationwide.

The same commitment

As we have grown, our standard has not changed. Fully licensed as a California Private Patrol Operator (PPO #121864), insured and bonded, with 24/7 live dispatch and documented accountability, we are focused on earning trust one client at a time.

We are proud of what we have built — and we are just getting started. If you are looking for a security partner who takes the work seriously, we would love to talk.
